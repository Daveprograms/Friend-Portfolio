My Latest Project

Subframe Modification Welding Jig

Sunday, Jan 14, 2023

Designed and manufactured a welding jig to meet GM-mandated FEA thresholds.

Click to View Detailed Information

Problem: To meet strict GM-mandated FEA thresholds and accommodate numerous welds and fasteners in the assembly, welds required a tolerance of +/-1mm to align motor mounts accurately without causing imbalanced load distribution and potential FEA failure.

Solution: Designed and manufactured a subframe welding jig, utilizing motor mounts as datum points and maximizing the use of stock and waterjet parts to ensure optimal load distribution and accuracy for the assembled jig.

Result: The welding jig was successfully manufactured and provided to the machine shop, enabling the completion of all necessary welds for the subframe modification. The motor mount holes aligned correctly, ensuring reliable assembly.

Tools Used: NX, AutoCAD, Waterjet Cutter, Hypermesh.

Accumulator Box Fan Shrouds

Sunday, Jan 14, 2023

Designed fan shrouds for cooling accumulator boxes.

Click to View Detailed Information

Problem: : Accumulator box requires fans for cooling cell segments but competition rules require accumulator boxes to be able to withstand 2 minutes of water-spray from all sides. Fans must be prepared for waterproof testing while maintaining optimal airflow to segments

Solution: Designed fan shroud variations using SolidWorks, testing 3D-printed prototypes for ingress protection and accumulator temperatures under load. Used PETG, silicone gasket, and nylon mesh to create final assembly for use on box

Result: : Accumulator box passed waterproofing test at competition and accumulator was able to run full load without thermal throttling, enabling top 10% scores at dynamic events and allowing team to secure 10th place finis

Tools Used: SolidWorks, Cura, 3D printing, Waterjet Cutter

Vehicle Computer Server Rack

Developed a server rack with sliding drawers and collapsible mounts for autonomous driving and control systems.

Click to View Detailed Information

Problem: Needed a mounting solution for autonomous driving, propulsion controls, communications, and datalogging computers that allows for easy access and removal.
